About Søndre Haugen Farm

Calm, nature, and tradition — a living farm estate for life's greatest moments.

An estate with character

Søndre Haugen lies at Svinndallinna 190, 1593 Svinndal in Våler, Østfold — surrounded by Norwegian cultural landscape: timber buildings, open fields, and the quiet only the countryside offers.

The property dates to 1760 and spans more than 10 mål (10,171 m²). The main house, stabbur, brew house, barn event hall, and large garage (2008) create a rare combination of history and modern use.

We combine heritage with professional operations: flexible indoor and outdoor space, a 12×6 m heated pool, and a clear ambition to deliver premium events and secure motorhome storage.

Host — Håvard Pedersen

Same authenticity on stage — calm on the farm

Håvard Pedersen is a Norwegian guitarist, singer, and entrepreneur — an endorsed artist with the Gretsch Company and Mezzabarba, with roots from Vardø and a base in Oslo. On stage he calls it Raw. Real. Live. On the farm it becomes Historic. Real. Yours.

After years of music, touring, and building brands, Håvard chose a physical place where culture, events, and hospitality meet — not a generic hall, but a farm with soul, run to the same independent standards as his music career.
